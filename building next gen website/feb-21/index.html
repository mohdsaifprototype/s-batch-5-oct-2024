Simple selector Part-3

We have many types of advance selectors such as

  1. Combinator selector

    When we combine two or more selector together to give condition/inheritance based styling then it's called combinator selector.

    Attribute selector

    Selector to give styling as per attribute and it's values. mostly used to differenciate element that have type attribute in them!

    Compound selector

    A combination of selector without spacing. Note: Compout selector is consist of only one element.
